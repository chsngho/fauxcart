# Project Phantom Buy 👻

### A Consumer-Side Behavioral Sandbox for Shifting Transaction Friction

Modern digital marketplaces are meticulously engineered using advanced platform architectures to minimize purchase friction. By exploiting cognitive biases through single-click ordering and immediate biometric verification, platforms maximize firm profit at the expense of rational consumer utility. 

**Project Phantom Buy** is an applied economics prototype designed to flip this dynamic. It serves as a **Consumer-Side Sandbox**—a safe, financially isolated environment that entirely replicates the frontend mechanics and high-dopamine reward loops of modern e-commerce pipelines, while introducing deliberate, ex-ante behavioral friction to mitigate impulsive spending.

---

## Theoretical Framework

This project stands at the intersection of two primary economic disciplines, modeled as a live case study in **Behavioral Industrial Organization (Behavioral IO)**:

### 1. Behavioral Economics
* **Hot-Cold Empathy Gaps:** Consumers in an affectively "hot state" (triggered by targeted marketing, gamified interfaces, or artificial scarcity) exhibit significant *Projection Bias*—overestimating the long-term utility of a transient craving.
* **Habit Loop Redirection:** Traditional financial advice relies on raw willpower, which induces cognitive fatigue. Phantom Buy uses *habit substitution*, capturing and neutralizing the behavioral routine (browsing, carting, authenticating) without capital loss.

### 2. Industrial Organization
* **Strategic Friction Manipulation:** Digital storefronts intentionally lower non-price transaction costs to extract consumer surplus. This sandbox introduces counter-frictions to restore consumer autonomy.
* **Information Asymmetry & Choice Architecture:** By altering the choice architecture at the critical moment of commitment, the sandbox re-calibrates market transparency in favor of the buyer.
